Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a laundry order? +
You can place an order by logging into your account and selecting the services you need.
How can I track my laundry status? +
Once your order is placed, you can track its status in your dashboard.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept M-Pesa payments through USSD integration.
What are your operating hours? +
We operate from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday to Saturday.
How does the USSD integration work? +
You can dial our USSD code, follow the prompts to place an order, and make payments via M-Pesa.
Can I schedule a pickup and delivery? +
Yes, our system allows you to schedule laundry pickup and delivery at your convenience.
What happens if I miss my delivery? +
If you miss a delivery, our support team will contact you to reschedule at a convenient time.
Do you offer express laundry services? +
Yes, we offer same-day express laundry services at an additional cost.
Is there a customer support team available? +
Yes, our support team is available during operating hours to assist with any queries or issues.
Can I cancel or modify my order? +
Yes, you can cancel or modify your order before it is processed through your account dashboard.
